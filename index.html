Soccer Training Drills

Daily practice guide

🦶

Left Foot

🧱

Wall Passes (Left Only)

Stand 2–3 feet from a wall and pass the ball using only your left foot. Focus on a firm, flat strike with the inside of your foot and control the return cleanly. Do sets of 30 passes, keeping a quick rhythm.

🔶

Cone Dribbling (Left Foot Only)

Set up 8–10 cones in a line, spaced about a yard apart. Weave through them using only your left foot, keeping the ball close with soft touches. Go slow at first, then build speed as your control improves.

🎯

Weak Foot Finishing

Set up shots from different angles around the box and finish every one with your left foot. Focus on placing the ball into the corners rather than blasting it. Aim for 20 quality shots per session.

🤹

Juggling Left Foot Only

Start by dropping the ball onto your left foot and try to keep it up as long as possible. Use the top of your foot (laces area) and keep your ankle locked. Try to beat your personal record every session.

⚡

Speed

🚀

10-Yard Explosive Starts

From a standing start, explode forward for 10 yards at maximum effort. Focus on driving your knees up and pumping your arms hard in the first three steps. Rest 30 seconds between reps and do 8–10 total.

🏆

Become the Fastest on Your Team

Basic steps to build real speed:

  1. Exercises
  2. Plyometrics: Broad Jumps, Hurdle Jumps, Bounding
  3. Running Form
  4. Sprint Training: 2x/wk Acceleration (10–20 yd) & Max Velocity (30–60 yd)
🏃‍♀️

40-Yard Sprint Intervals

Sprint 40 yards at full speed, then walk back to the start line to recover. Keep your form tight — chest up, arms driving, toes pointing forward. Do 6–8 reps with full recovery between each one.

↔️

Lateral Shuffle + Sprint

Shuffle laterally for 5 yards, then explode into a forward sprint for 10 yards. Stay low during the shuffle with your weight on the balls of your feet. Alternate directions and do 8 reps total.

🔥

High Knees + Burst

Do high knees in place for 5 seconds, driving your knees as high and fast as possible. On a signal, burst into a 10-yard sprint. This trains your ability to accelerate out of quick footwork.

💥

Shot Power

🎯

Accuracy Game

Players are 12 yards apart with a 1-yard gate in the middle. There must be a line between players.

  • Hit a cone = 1 point
  • Miss the gate = 2 points
  • Keep your pass touching the center line = 1 point

First player to 6 points loses.

  • Start your approach directly behind the ball
  • Keep your passing foot perpendicular to the passing line at contact
  • Drive your ankle bone straight down the target line through the ball
  • Return your passing foot straight back to the ground instead of allowing it to swing upward — this keeps the ball driven cleanly along the ground

Once players become consistent, increase the challenge by progressing into finishing — slotting the ball into the corner as if beating a goalkeeper in a 1v1. The best training gives them a game that makes the correct technique the easiest way to win.

⚽

Standing Power Shots

Place the ball dead still and strike it as hard as you can with your laces from a standing position. Plant your non-kicking foot beside the ball and swing through completely. Focus on clean contact over aim.

👟

One-Step Power Shots

Take just one step before striking the ball with maximum power. This isolates your kicking technique and forces you to generate power efficiently. Lock your ankle and follow through toward the target.

🔄

Hip Rotation Drill

Practice the kicking motion without a ball, focusing on rotating your hips fully through the strike. Your hips should open up and drive forward — this is where real shot power comes from. Do 15 slow reps per side.

🥾

Laces-Only Shooting

Strike every shot using only the top of your foot (laces). Keep your toe pointed down, ankle locked, and lean slightly over the ball. This builds the habit of hitting clean, powerful driven shots.

Train hard. Stay consistent. Get better every day.
